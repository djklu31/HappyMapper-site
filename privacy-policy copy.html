Privacy Policy

HappyMapper · Last updated: May 13, 2026

What we do not collect

HappyMapper does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We do not track your usage, location, behavior, or device identifiers. We do not use analytics tools, advertising SDKs, or any third-party data collection services.

Camera roll and photo library

If you choose to import photos or videos from your photo library, that media is accessed locally on your device only. It is never uploaded to any server or shared with any third party. Access is only requested when you actively choose to import media.

Internet access and third-party video search

HappyMapper connects to the internet only when you use the video or GIF search feature. Searches are sent to Pexels, Pixabay, and Giphy using their respective APIs. These services may log search queries per their own privacy policies. We do not store your search queries and no personal information is included in these requests.

Purchases

If you purchase a Premium subscription or lifetime access, payment is handled entirely by Apple via StoreKit. We never see, access, or store your payment information.

Data storage

All app data including your saved mappings, shapes, and settings is stored locally on your device. Nothing is synced to external servers or cloud services.

Children

HappyMapper is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change this policy in a meaningful way, we will update the date below and note the change in the app's release notes.
